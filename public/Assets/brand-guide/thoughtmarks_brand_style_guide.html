Introduction

Thoughtmarks is a cognitive capture tool designed for people who think faster than they can organize. We're not another note-taking app. We're the app that catches ideas before they vanish.

This brand guide establishes the visual and verbal standards that ensure Thoughtmarks remains recognizable, consistent, and true to its core identity across all touchpoints.

Brand Essence

What We Are: A voice-first cognitive capture tool with AI organization

What We're Not: Another note-taking app, voice memo app, or productivity system

Brand Personality: Smart but not pretentious, fast and efficient, supportive of chaos

Core Positioning

"The app for people who think faster than they can organize. Capture ideas at the speed of life. AI organizes them automatically. Find anything with semantic search. Your brain can finally stop trying to remember everything."

Color Usage

Primary Yellow (#FEFF00)

This is our brand identifier. Use it boldly but sparingly.

  • DO: Use for CTAs, tagline, bookmark icon, accent elements
  • DO: Use on black backgrounds for maximum impact
  • DO: Keep usage to less than 10% of total composition
  • DON'T: Use for large background areas (causes eye strain)
  • DON'T: Use for body text (readability issues)
  • DON'T: Pair with other bright saturated colors

Black & Dark Backgrounds

Dark mode is our default. It's not an option, it's the brand.

  • Primary background: Deep Black (#000000)
  • Card surfaces: Shark (#1A1A1E)
  • Alternative backgrounds: Cod Gray (#181818)

Gray Tones

Use for structure, secondary elements, and inactive states.

  • Bombay (#B3B9C0): Circuit traces, brain outline, secondary UI
  • Oslo Gray (#83868B): Tertiary text, inactive elements

Accessibility Notes

  • Yellow on black: AAA contrast (21:1 ratio)
  • Yellow on Shark: AA contrast for large text
  • Always test yellow text on gray backgrounds before use
  • Consider colorblind users: yellow/black works for most types

Typography Hierarchy

Type Scale

Element Font Weight Size Case
Wordmark Lato Black (900) Variable ALL CAPS
Tagline Marydale Regular Variable lowercase
H1 Headlines Lato Black (900) 36-48pt ALL CAPS
H2 Subheads Lato Bold (700) 24-32pt Sentence case
H3 Tertiary Lato Bold (700) 16-20pt Sentence case
Body Text Lato Regular (400) 11-14pt Sentence case
UI Labels Lato Regular (400) 10-12pt Sentence case
Captions Lato Light (300) 9-11pt Sentence case

Typography Rules

  • ALL CAPS only for wordmark and major headlines
  • Sentence case for most content (more approachable)
  • Line height: 1.5-1.6 for body text, 1.2 for headlines
  • Letter spacing: Slightly negative (-1 to -2px) for large headlines
  • Never center-align body text
  • Generous white space - let the copy breathe

  • Icon must be recognizable at 32x32px minimum
  • Bookmark flag must remain yellow in all variations
  • Brain outline should be visible against background
  • Don't add additional elements to the icon
  • Don't alter the brain or bookmark proportions

Voice & Messaging

Brand Voice

  • Direct: Get to the point, no fluff
  • Energetic: Active verbs, punchy sentences
  • Honest: Acknowledge real problems, no BS
  • Smart: Intelligent but not pretentious
  • Supportive: We get it, chaos is normal

What We Say

  • "Capture brilliant ideas"
  • "Stay in flow"
  • "Your digital brain"
  • "Voice in. Organized out."
  • "Find the thing you half remember"

What We Never Say

  • "Maximize productivity" (corporate speak)
  • "Synergize" (meaningless jargon)
  • "Revolutionary transformation" (overpromising)
  • "Hack your brain" (guru nonsense)
  • "Be your best self" (preachy)

Core Messages

How It Works:

  1. Capture - voice or text in seconds
  2. Organize - auto tags, categories, connections
  3. Discover - search by words or meaning

Value Props:

  • Effortless Capture (Siri integrated, voice-to-text)
  • Automatic Structure (AI tags, no folders, no chores)
  • Smart Search (semantic + keyword search)
  • Privacy First (lock on device, encrypted sync optional)

Applications

Social Media

Instagram Stories/Reels (9:16)

  • Dimensions: 1080x1920px
  • Background: Dark with blurred brain pattern
  • Card: Glass morphism effect, max 70% height
  • Layout: Avatar + Title (top), Content (middle), Branding (bottom)
  • Tagline: Always yellow, always at bottom

Feed Posts (1:1)

  • Dimensions: 1080x1080px
  • Style: Similar treatment, balanced composition
  • Background: Dark or very dark gray
  • Text: High contrast white on dark

Email Templates

  • Header: Tagline + Wordmark on dark background
  • Body: White background, black text, Lato Regular
  • CTAs: Yellow buttons with black text
  • Footer: Dark background, minimal information

Presentation Templates

  • Title slides: Dark background, yellow accents
  • Content slides: Dark or light depending on readability
  • Always include tagline or wordmark in footer
  • Use Lato throughout, maintain hierarchy

Print Materials

  • Business cards: Dark background, minimal design
  • Letterhead: Simple wordmark, mostly white space
  • Note: Yellow is ink-intensive, test on paper stocks
  • Consider spot color for brand consistency

Technical Specifications

Color Profiles

Application Color Space Notes
iOS App Display P3 Wider gamut, more vibrant yellow
Web sRGB Standard web color space
Print CMYK Test yellow on paper stock first
Social Media sRGB 72 DPI acceptable

File Formats

  • Web: SVG for logos, PNG for raster images
  • iOS: PNG at @2x and @3x, PDF for vectors
  • Print: PDF or EPS with embedded fonts
  • Social: PNG or JPG, RGB color mode

Resolution Standards

  • Web: 2x and 3x for Retina displays
  • Print: 300 DPI minimum
  • iOS: @2x and @3x asset catalogs
  • Social: Platform-specific requirements

Font Files

  • Lato: Available on Google Fonts (open source)
  • Marydale: Licensed font (verify usage rights)
  • Always embed fonts in PDFs and presentations
  • Use web fonts (WOFF2) for website implementations
