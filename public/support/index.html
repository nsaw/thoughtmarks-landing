Frequently Asked Questions

What is Thoughtmarks?

Thoughtmarks is an AI-powered note-taking app designed for capturing brilliant ideas at the speed of life. With voice-first design and intelligent organization, you never lose another breakthrough moment.

How does voice recording work?

Tap the microphone, speak your thought, tap again to stop. AI transcribes your voice with 95%+ accuracy. Works offline and syncs when you're back online. Available on iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. We use AES-256 encryption, hardware-backed keychain storage, and offer on-device AI processing. Your data never leaves your device unless you choose cloud features. GDPR & CCPA compliant.

Does it work offline?

Yes! Full offline functionality including voice recording, note creation, search, and organization. Everything syncs automatically when you're back online.

How much does it cost?

Thoughtmarks offers a free tier with core features. Premium subscription ($4.99/month or $39.99/year) includes advanced AI, cloud backup, and unlimited voice recording. Limited time: Premium features free during beta.

Can I export my data?

Yes! Export all your data in JSON, Markdown, or CSV format. Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Data ‚Üí Export My Data. Full data portability guaranteed under GDPR & CCPA.
