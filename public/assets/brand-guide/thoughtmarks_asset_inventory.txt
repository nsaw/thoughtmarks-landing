ASSET OPTIMIZATION NOTES

Cache Busting
  - Font URLs include ?cb=v4 parameter
  - Image URLs may include ?cb=v4 parameter
  - See src/styles/fonts.css and src/components/astro/PhoneMockup.astro
  
File Formats
  - PNG: Screenshots, icons, graphics with transparency
  - JPG: Social share cards, photos
  - SVG: Scalable graphics (logos, icons)
  - TTF: Font files (self-hosted)
  
Resolution Standards
  - iOS icons: @2x and @3x variants for Retina
  - Web images: Responsive with srcset where appropriate
  - Social media: Platform-specific dimensions (1080x1920 for Instagram)

Color Space
  - iOS icons: Display P3 (wider gamut)
  - Web assets: sRGB
  - Social media: sRGB
